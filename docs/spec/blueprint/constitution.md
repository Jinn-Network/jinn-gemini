# Principles

### Decouple logic from execution
Deploy product logic to shared, generic execution environments. Avoid bespoke infra and tokenomics just to run. Reduce builder complexity by separating what code does from how and where it runs.

### Humans set ends; agents discover means
Decouple intent from logic. Users specify outcomes; systems learn plans. Respect The Bitter Lesson: favor learning and self-directed improvement over hand-authored procedures. Humans define goals and constraints; agents own execution.

### Autonomy over scope; push to the edge
Choose end-to-end autonomy even if it reduces initial capability. Human-in-the-loop execution is a long-term bottleneck; minimize it. Run compute and orchestration at the edges via independent operators to maximize scale, resilience, and innovation.

### Respect the flows
The most capable agentic AI systems sit on the most fluid economic rails. Agents will be the dominant consumers of AI inference and require large, reliable capital pipes. Build on crypto primitives to expand and automate funding; in the early phase, design compelling opportunities for traders to seed flow.
