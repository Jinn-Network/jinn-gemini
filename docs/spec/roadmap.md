# Roadmap

## Current Cycle (January - February 2026)

The team is focused on three key initiatives:

### Infinitely Running Autonomous Business
Implementing long-running, self-sustaining ventures that operate continuously. This includes cyclic job execution for ventures that never stop, continuous measurement scheduling for ongoing invariant assessment, and self-healing infrastructure that adapts to failures.

### Jinn Staking Contract Deployment
Deploying and configuring the Jinn-specific staking contract on Base mainnet. This establishes the economic foundation for independent venture operation, enabling veOLAS holders to direct emissions to Jinn ventures.

### Documentation & Developer Experience
Creating modern, searchable documentation with Fumadocs and ShadCN theming. Improving the onboarding experience for new operators and launchers with comprehensive guides and live examples.

---

## Recently Completed

### Protocol Verification & Evaluation ✅
Established verification infrastructure for autonomous protocol self-assessment. Created canonical protocol descriptions, built automated validation flows, and developed the four-type invariant schema (FLOOR, CEILING, RANGE, BOOLEAN) for precise success criteria measurement.

### Template Marketplace ✅
Launched x402-powered template marketplace enabling reusable job workflows. Templates surface historical cost data for transparent pricing and include OutputSpec for deterministic response extraction. Available at [explorer.jinn.network/templates](https://explorer.jinn.network/templates).

### Universal Mech Indexing ✅
Upgraded Ponder indexer to global explorer architecture. All Jinn network requests are now indexed regardless of mech address, with networkId filtering at the IPFS metadata level. The explorer shows activity across all participating operators.

### Blog Growth Product ✅
Shipped the Blog Growth template with auto-provisioning infrastructure. Ventures can now launch autonomous content operations with built-in analytics, multi-channel distribution, and adaptive strategy optimization.
