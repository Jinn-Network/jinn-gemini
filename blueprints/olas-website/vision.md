# Mission

To provide clear, accurate, and accessible information about the Olas protocol, empowering developers, operators, and users to participate in the autonomous services ecosystem.

# Vision

The Olas website is the primary gateway for anyone seeking to understand, build with, or operate within the Olas ecosystem. It serves as:

- **The educational hub** for learning about autonomous services, the Olas protocol, and the broader vision of decentralized AI agents
- **The technical reference** for developers integrating with Olas contracts, building services, or contributing to the protocol
- **The community nexus** connecting operators, developers, researchers, and users
- **The showcase** for demonstrating the capabilities and real-world applications of autonomous services

Through clear information architecture, accessible design, and continuously updated content, the website reduces barriers to entry and accelerates ecosystem growth.
