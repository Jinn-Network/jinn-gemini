## What we're thinking about

We're scaling from 2 services to 100. Right now we're capped at ~120 deliveries/day but have work for many more. Batch-provisioning services to fill the staking contract, and building a fleet profitability dashboard to make sure rewards cover inference and gas.

We're also introducing a "feed" convention so content ventures mark their outputs as consumable streams. This lets agents compose content across ventures — e.g., a Telegram bot that reads multiple research feeds and posts summaries automatically.
