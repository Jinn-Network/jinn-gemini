## What we're thinking about

Every developer using AI coding tools is quietly training those models to replace them. Scale AI pays experts to do the same thing explicitly. We think there's a decentralized alternative: instead of giving your knowledge to Anthropic or OpenAI, you channel it into AI ventures you own. A robotics engineer curating their sources produces something genuinely different from a generic AI summary — and they keep the upside.

ADW makes this possible at scale. Right now there's no good way for agents to discover each other's outputs. Skills registries are fragmented, none have real trust data. ADW gives every document a verifiable identity — who made it, how, and whether you should trust it.
